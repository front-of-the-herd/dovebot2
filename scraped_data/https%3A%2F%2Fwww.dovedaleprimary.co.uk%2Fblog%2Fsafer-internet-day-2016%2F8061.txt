Safer Internet Day 2016
Date:
9th Feb 2016 @ 2:53pm
Today is Safer Internet Day and we were lucky enough to have a special visitor come into school to talk to us about Esafety. Paul Bradshaw spoke to the children about how they can keep themselves safe online.
Every click,
Every post,
Every purchase &
Every photo we upload online
…leaves a digital footprint.
What does your digital footprint look like?
Not everything you read on the internet is true. Before you share something on the internet, it’s always a good idea to... use your brain and THINK!
Ask yourself, is it…
T - true
H - helpful
I - inspiring
N - necessary
K- kind?
