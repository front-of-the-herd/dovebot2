Week ending 7th December 2018
Date:
7th Dec 2018 @ 2:46pm
This week in Year 5, we have been working extremely hard and enjoying the following activities: -
Maths – Reading and interpreting tables.
English – Instructions.
R.E – Is the Christmas story true?
Art – Designing rockets based on the art work of Peter Thorpe
P.E – Basketball
Music and ICT
Science – Observing differences involving mass as we develop and drawing line graphs
Geography – A local redevelopment (Redrow housing) – writing a letter
Again, another amazing week we have had. We were extremely impressed with the children’s work. Their emails to the city council were extremely persuasive! Save Our School Field!!
We cannot wait until next week where we will be enjoying the following lessons:
Maths – Squared, cubed and Prime numbers.
English – SPAG focus.
Assessments – Reading, spelling and SPAG tests
D.T – Investigating and designing bird hides
P.E – Basketball
Music and ICT
A huge thank you for your continuing support.
Please ensure that you listen to your child read at least 3 times a week.
Please make sure that your child brings in their reading book and record card every day.
Remember to use your Mathletics account and have fun with maths!!!
