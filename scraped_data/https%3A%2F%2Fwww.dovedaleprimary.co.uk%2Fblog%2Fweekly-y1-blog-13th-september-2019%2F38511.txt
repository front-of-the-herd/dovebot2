Weekly Y1 Blog 13th September 2019
Date:
12th Sep 2019 @ 7:39pm
Welcome to our blog. We update this weekly to let you know what we have been working on and also any information you may need for the up coming week.
This week we have been:
Maths –  looking a place value. We have been exploring one more than a given number.
English -  We looked at the suffix 'est' and recapped over capital letter, full stop and finger spaces. We have also introduced the word 'adjective'
Science – We have explored the terms 'Omnivore, Herbivore and Carnivore'
Computing - We have exploring how to control a beebot by using only code.
Next week we will be:
Maths –  Exploring one less, greater than and less than.
English - We will be predicting our new text.
ICT - Continuing to work with Beebots
Science -  Classification of animals
Homework this week - Children have weekly spellings and assigned Mathletics homework. Check the login details in their reading diaries.
Please continue logging on to Oxford Reading Buddy.
Have a lovely weekend!
