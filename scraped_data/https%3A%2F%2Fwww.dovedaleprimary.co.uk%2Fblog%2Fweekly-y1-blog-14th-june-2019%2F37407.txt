Weekly Y1 Blog 14th June 2019
Date:
14th Jun 2019 @ 2:33pm
This week we have been:
Maths- looking at place value of 2 digit number up to 100. Using part whole model to partition
English- looking at prefix un, exploring new vocabulary for our book
Geography-locating local beaches on a map and talking about the seaside
RE - Talking about the Jewish faith
Next week we will be:
Maths- using the vocabulary of most and least to order amounts.
English- exploring exciting vocabulary
Geography- We will be placing our photographs on the map and locating where they have been taken.
We will be exploring the term 'Refugee'.
We will be looking a week 6 spellings this week.
Please continue logging on to Mathletics.
Have a lovely weekend!
