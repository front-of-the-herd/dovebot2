Weekly Y1 Blog 21st June 2019
Date:
21st Jun 2019 @ 2:23pm
This week we have been:
Maths- exploring the term ‘more’ and ‘less’ when comparing  numbers and objects.
English-  We finished our wonderful story ‘Ocean meets Sky’ and used the suffix ed.
Geography- We located local beaches on a big class map.
Punch and Judy day was great fun! The children enjoyed experiences a real Punch and Judy show!
Next week we will be:
Maths- Exploring money
English- Starting a new text and talking about conjunctions.
Geography- labelling the features of a seaside
Science – Cress experiment
Learn and Explore – Tuesday 25
th
June 2pm-3pm
Sports Day - Friday 28st June
We will be looking a week 7 spellings this week.
Please continue logging on to Mathletics.
Have a lovely weekend!
