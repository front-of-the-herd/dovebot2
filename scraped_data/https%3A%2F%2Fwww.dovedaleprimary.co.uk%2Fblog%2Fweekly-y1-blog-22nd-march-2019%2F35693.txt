Weekly Y1 Blog 22nd March 2019
Date:
21st Mar 2019 @ 4:06pm
This week we have been:
Maths –   Measuring weight using non-standard units of measure. Using key vocabulary such as mass, weight, heavier and lighter.
English -  Explored how to use exclamation marks and question marks.
RE - We discussed how we would welcome Jesus into Liverpool now.
Cricket - We continued with our Cricket coaching and all remembered the work 'Wicket'.
Geography - Explored the continent of Oceania and reported on a Whale Shark sighting.
Next Week we will be:
Maths –   Exploring capacity and volume (it is going to be a messy week!)
English - Thinking about climate change and what consequences it has on our animals.
RE - Looking at the Easter Story.
Cricket - We will be continuing with our Cricket coaching
Geography - Exploring the continent of Africa
Science - Comparing the different Seasons
Computing -Exploring the 'Repeat' button in Scratch.
Please continue logging on to Mathletics.
We will be looking a week 4 spellings this week.
Please continue logging on to Mathletics.
Have a lovely weekend!
