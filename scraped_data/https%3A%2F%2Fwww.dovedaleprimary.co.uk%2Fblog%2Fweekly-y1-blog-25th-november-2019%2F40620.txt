Weekly Y1 Blog 25th November 2019
Date:
22nd Nov 2019 @ 12:50pm
This week we have been:
Maths - Counting on to find a missing number
English – Talking about seasons
Art - Observational drawing of toys from the past and mark making
RE- Beginning to talk about the Christmas story
ICT - Creating a video about healthy eating
Next week we will be:
Maths - Starting to look at subtraction
English – Exploring our new text and learning how to up level our writing
History - Exploring what school was like in the past.
RE- Continuing to look at the Christmas story.
ICT - Talking about algorithms
Homework this week - Children have weekly spellings and assigned Mathletics homework. Check the login details in their reading diaries.
Children also have a reading homework.
Please continue logging on to Oxford Reading Buddy.
We are on week 4 for spellings!
Have a lovely weekend!
