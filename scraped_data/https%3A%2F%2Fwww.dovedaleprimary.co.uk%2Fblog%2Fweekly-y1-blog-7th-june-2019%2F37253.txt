Weekly Y1 Blog 7th June 2019
Date:
7th Jun 2019 @ 1:13pm
This week we have been:
Maths –  Exploring more directional language. Using the language of left, right, half and quarter turn.
English – Exploring our new book. We have predicted what the front cover looked like just from the title and then predicted what the might happen in the story.
Geography – We discussed our prior knowledge of the seaside.
Thank you for all your support this week. We had a great Seaside Day!
Next week we will:
Maths- looking at place value of 2 digit number up to 100. Using part whole model to partition
English- looking at prefix un, exploring new vocabulary for our book
Geography-locating local beaches on a map
Computing - Understanding that some actions may hurt others
We will be looking a week 5 spellings this week.
Please continue logging on to Mathletics.
Have a lovely weekend!
