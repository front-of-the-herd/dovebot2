Weekly Y1 Blog 9th November 2018
Date:
8th Nov 2018 @ 9:04pm
We have had a wonderful week.
We have been learning:
Maths - Number bonds to 10 and 20. We have been solving tricky problems such as: 4+2 < 4+____
English - We have been continuing to look at the story 'The Little Red Hen'. We used our imagination to change the character and setting to create our own story.
History - We have been talking about Rememberance Sunday.
RE - We have been celebrating Diwali and also we started talking about the Christmas Story.
We have also been focusing on counting backwards in 2s. We will continue to practise this.
We have been looking at the words - the,do, a
Next week we will be:
Maths – We will exploring subtraction
English - Using the suffix 'ed'
Geography - beginning to explore our local area
ICT - We will be introducing Scratch
Have a lovely weekend!
