Weekly Y3 Blog 26.4.19
Date:
26th Apr 2019 @ 3:51pm
This week in Year 3, we have been working extremely hard and enjoying the following activities:
Maths:
Reading time to the nearest 15 minutes
Reading time to the nearest 5 minutes
English – We have started our new topic of instructions. This week, we made a smoothie cookery video which children will then turn into a set of instructions.
Geography – Drawing floor plans
Science – Animals including Humans – What do we need to survive?
Music, Computing and French
P.E.
Next week, we shall be enjoying the following activities:
Maths:
Reading time to the nearest 1 minute past the hour
Reading time to the nearest 1 minute to the hour
A range of problems around reading the time to the nearest minute
English – We shall continue our instructions topic next week and start to write the set of instructions that you can follow to make an ‘interesting’ smoothie.
Geography – Drawing floor plans and maps
Science – Animals including Humans – What do we need to stay healthy?
Music, Computing and French
P.E.
