Weekly Y3 Blog 28.6.19
Date:
28th Jun 2019 @ 8:24am
This week in Year 3, we have been working extremely hard and enjoying the following activities:
Maths - Sorting 3D shapes based on their properties and making a range of 3D shapes. Check out or Twitter page to see the range of 3D shapes we created.
English – Reading Assessments
Geography – Creating our own weather reports using a range of related vocabulary
Science – Science Assessments
Music, Computing and French
P.E.
PSHE – Change Me
Sports Day - What a great day we had! Well done to all the children who took part and everyone who came to watch! Check out our Twitter page for some pictures of the day.
Next week, we shall be enjoying the following activities:
Maths  - Reading scales for mass, reading scales for capacity and mass problem solving
Maths Party Day
English – We shall we focusing on cross curricular writing this week in English. We shall write a weather using a range of geography vocabulary.
Music, Computing and French
P.E.
PSHE – Change Me
D.T – Shell structures – We shall start our research on shell structures.
