Weekly Y3 Blog 7.6.19
Date:
7th Jun 2019 @ 12:55pm
This week in Year 3, we have been working extremely hard and enjoying the following activities:
Maths - Investigating horizontal, vertical, parallel and perpendicular lines
English – Alternative fairy tales – The True Story of the Three Little Pigs
History – Ancient Egyptian Gods
Science  - Animals including Humans investigation
Music, Computing and French
P.E.
Reading – Performance pottery - Down Behind the Dustbin by Michael Rosen
Spelling – Dictionary work
Next week, we shall be enjoying the following activities:
Maths  - Describing turns and angles, classifying shapes based on their properties, comparing angles within shapes and drawing shapes accurately
English – We shall continue our work on alternative fairy tales and The True Story of the Three Little Pigs. Next week, we shall write a statement from the perspective of the wolf regarding his versions of events.
Geography – Locating some of the world’s climate zones on a globe
Science – Human body investigation
Spelling – Homophones
Reading – Performance pottery - Down Behind the Dustbin by Michael Rosen
Music, Computing and French
P.E.
