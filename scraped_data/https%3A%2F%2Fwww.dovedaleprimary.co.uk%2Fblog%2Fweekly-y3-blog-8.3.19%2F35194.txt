Weekly Y3 Blog 8.3.19
Date:
7th Mar 2019 @ 8:44am
This week in Year 3, we have been working extremely hard and enjoying the following activities:
Maths – We have focused on the following objectives:
Ordering fractions
Adding fractions
Subtracting fractions
Equivalent fractions
English – After researching and discovering the story/history of LEGO we have started to write an explanation text about the Lego story.
Geography – We used atlases and digital maps to locate countries around the world.
Science – Light – We had visitors from the University of Liverpool who talked to us and showed us the importance of bacteria
P.E – Games
Music, PSHE and French
World Book Day - We took par in lots of fun and exciting activates.
Next week, we shall be enjoying the following activities:
Maths - We shall start our new topic of fractions. During this week, we will focus on the following objectives:
Fraction investigation – Is it less than a half, the same as a half or more than a half?
Equivalent fraction problems
Finding fractions of number
English – We will continue to write our explanation of the LEGO story.
Geography – Using
co-ordinates to navigate
Science – Light – What object would make a good shadow?
Music, Computing and French
P.E.
R.E – The Easter Story
