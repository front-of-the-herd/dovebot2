Weekly Y4 Blog 1.3.19
Date:
1st Feb 2019 @ 1:16pm
This week in Year 4, we have been working extremely hard once again. We have enjoyed learning about the following topics:
Maths – using the bus stop method for division, using our 11 and 12 times tables and multiplying 3 digits.
English – Leaflets – creating our own leaflet for our school.
History – Boudicca – who was she? Why is she important?
Science – Teeth investigation – what do your teeth do to the food you eat?
PE – Fitness
French – All about me – What is your name? How old are you?
Music and ICT
Next week is another fun filled week, were we will be learning about:
Maths – factors and area.
English – Information texts – qualities of a Roman soldier.
History – The Roman Army
Science – Food chains, predators and prey.
PE – Fitness
French – Games and songs
Music and ICT
We also have a special Rainbow Smiles workshop for PHSE.
