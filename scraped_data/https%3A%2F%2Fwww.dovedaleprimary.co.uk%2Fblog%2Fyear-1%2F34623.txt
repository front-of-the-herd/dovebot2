Weekly Y1 Blog 8th February  2019
Date:
8th Feb 2019 @ 2:54pm
We have had a very busy week in Year 1
This week:
Maths – We are looked at fact families and compared number sentences using the inequality symbol.
English – Used the suffix 'ed' and the conjunction 'because' to describe the Snipe.
Chinese New Year - We celebrated Chinese New Year through a variety of craft, dancing and stories.
Safer Internet Day - We know now how to stay safe on the Internet. We created our own internet.
Next week:
Maths – Place value of numbers up to 50 and finding one more and one less.
English -  Our weekly focus words are:
where, who, why
Using a conjunction to join sentences
Science - Exploring suitable materials for a Polar Explorer.
Art - Using a variety of skills to join materials
Please practise Week 5 spellings in the homework book and continue logging on to Mathletics.
Have a lovely weekend.
