Date:
17th Mar 2019 @ 8:15am
It has been another busy week in Year 4, this is what we have been learning:
Maths – Equivalent fractions
English – Writing Cinquain and Kenning poems.
Geography – Using our map skills and an atlas to find out about Rome.
Science –Melting and Freezing.
RE– All about forgiveness.
PE – Dance
Music and ICT
Next week will be as busy as ever and we will continue to build on the skills and knowledge we have learnt this week.
English – Writing a character description.
Maths – Continuing equivalent fractions.
Geography – continuing to develop of map skills
Science – Finding out about gases.
RE – Forgiveness in the Easter story
PE – Dance
Music and ICT
Have a great weekend, The Year 4 Team
