Year 4 Weekly Blog - 23.5.19
Date:
23rd May 2019 @ 2:00pm
Once again we are at the end of another busy half term. We had a fabulous time at Quarry Bank Mill and have enjoyed our Cotton trade topic in History. Also this week we have been learning about:
English –Cold write – Recount
Maths – Money.
History – Trip to Quarry Bank Mill
Science –Conductors and Insulators.
PE – Athletics
Music and ICT
After the holiday will be entering our final half term in Year 4 and will be as busy as ever. We will be learning about:
English – Continuing to look at our text Street Child
Maths –Statistics.
Science – Continuing to investigate circuits and introducing our new equipment.
PE – Ball skills
Music and ICT
Have a great half term holiday,
The Year 4 Team
