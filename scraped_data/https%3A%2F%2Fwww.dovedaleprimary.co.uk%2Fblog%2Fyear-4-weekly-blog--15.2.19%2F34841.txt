Year 4 Weekly Blog- 15.2.19
Date:
15th Feb 2019 @ 4:24pm
It has been a very busy half term in Year 4 and this week has been no exception:
Next week is our last week before half term and we are as busy as ever:
Maths –finding out about fractions.
English – Information texts – completing a job application to be a Roman soldier.
History – The Roman Army
Science –Finding out more about food chains.
PE – Fitness
French – Continuing some simple.
Music and ICT
After half term and we will be completing some assessments and visiting Chester as part of our History topic:
Maths –Area, multiplication and division assessments. Times tables
English –Cold write linked to the Romans.
History – School trip to Chester
Science – Introducing States of Matter.
PE – Dance
French –Learning simple phrases you could use on holiday in France.
Music and ICT
Hope you all have a lovely half term.
The Year 4 Team
