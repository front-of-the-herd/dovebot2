Year 5 blog - week ending 11th January
Date:
11th Jan 2019 @ 1:27pm
This week in Year 5, we have been working extremely hard and enjoying the following activities: -
Maths – Perimeter of rectangles and compound shapes.
English – Recounts – The Mystery of The Missing Computer.
History – Why did The Vikings come to Britain?
Science – Forces. What makes an object fall to Earth?
P.E – Invasion games
Jigsaw – What do I want to be when I grow up?
Swimming – Developing confidence and water skills
Music and ICT
Happy New Year from everyone in Year 5!
Again, another amazing week we have had. We were extremely impressed with the children’s work. We are so glad that the mystery was solved about the missing computer. It was all a misunderstanding.
We cannot wait until next week where we will be enjoying the following lessons:
Maths – Area of rectangles and compound shapes.
English – Recounts – Biographiy of John Lennon.
History – Timeline
Science – Forces. Understanding what makes things move.
P.E – Invasion games
Jigsaw – Dreams and goals. Looking at jobs.
Swimming – Developing confidence and water skills
Music and ICT
A huge thank you for your continuing support.
Please ensure that you listen to your child read at least 3 times a week.
Please make sure that your child brings in their reading book and record card every day.
Don’t forget your swimming cap.
Remember to use your Mathletics account and have fun with maths!!!
