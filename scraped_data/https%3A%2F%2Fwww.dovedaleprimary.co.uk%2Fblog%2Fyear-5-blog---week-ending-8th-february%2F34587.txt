Year 5 blog - week ending 8th February
Date:
8th Feb 2019 @ 9:01am
This week in Year 5, we have been working extremely hard and enjoying the following activities: -
Maths – Equivalent fractions and looking at life skills with Barclays bank
English – Recounts – Sector 7. An unusual school trip.
Art – Viking Long ships – shields and figure head designs.
Science – Forces. Streamlining.
P.E – Invasion games
Swimming – Developing confidence and water skills
Music and ICT
Again, another superb week we have had. A huge thank you to Barclays bank for giving up their time. The children looked at budgeting and prioritising money. A real eye opener. We really appreciate it. Thank you!
We cannot wait until next week where we will be enjoying the following lessons:
Maths – Fractions and assessment
English – Commas and word classes
Writing – Cold write
Science – Water resistance and assessment
P.E – Invasion games
Swimming – Developing confidence and water skills
Music and ICT
French – On the way to school
A huge thank you for your continuing support.
Please ensure that you listen to your child read at least 3 times a week.
Please make sure that your child brings in their reading book and record card every day.
Don’t forget your swimming cap.
Remember to use your Mathletics account and have fun with maths!!!
Try out our new app to help you with your Times Tables! Awake that inner rock god!
